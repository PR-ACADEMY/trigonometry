Important Points in Trigonometry

üîë De Moivre's Theorem Applications

De Moivre's Theorem is essential for finding powers and roots of complex numbers, simplifying trigonometric expressions, and deriving multiple-angle formulas.

üîë Hyperbolic Function Identities

Key identities include: cosh2x - sinh2x = 1, sinh(2x) = 2 sinh x cosh x, and cosh(2x) = cosh2x + sinh2x.

üîë Trigonometric Summation Formulas

üîë Multiple Angle Expansions

Expansions of cos nŒ∏ and sin nŒ∏ can be obtained using De Moivre's Theorem and the binomial theorem, or by using recurrence relations.
